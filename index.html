Research

My work lies at the interface of quantum field theory, scattering amplitudes, and non-perturbative methods for strongly interacting systems.

  • S-matrix Bootstrap and non-perturbative constraints on QFT.
  • Applications to QCD, hadron spectroscopy, and cross-section bounds.
  • Constraints on quantum gravity and UV completions of the Standard Model.

Open Questions & Notes

A (very incomplete) list of questions and directions I find exciting:

  • Bootstrap constraints on exotic hadrons and multi-quark states.
  • Constraints on higher-derivative corrections (e.g. R4) in quantum gravity from S-matrix positivity.
